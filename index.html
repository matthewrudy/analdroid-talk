My App

Learn Chinese Vocabulary bit.ly/learnchineseapp

What do I want to track?

Popularity

  • How many people are *actually* using it?
  • How many cards do they look at?
  • What level do they use?

Behaviour

  • Buttons or Swipe?
  • Does anyone press the Random button?

The API

Page Views

        trackPageView(
          "/LearnChineseActivity");
          
        trackPageView(
          "/FlashCardActivity
            /Level/HSK1
              /Card/1");

Events

        trackEvent(
          "LearnChineseActivity",
            "Start",             
              "HSK1", 1);        
        
        trackEvent(
          "FlashCardActivity",
            "Swipe",
              "Next", 1);
        or
            "Click",
              "Random", 1);

The Results

Page Views

  • Fair number of users
  • Most look at HSK1, browse a few words, then leave
  • Longtail (~30 per week) views on other words

Events

  • Most people Click ~80%
  • Random is pretty popular ~30%
  • Previous is less so ~15%
