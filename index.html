What do I want to track?

Usage Stats

  • How many people are using it
  • How much do they use it?
  • How many cards do they look at?
  • What levels are popular?

Behaviour

  • Buttons or Swipe?
  • "Random" does it get used?

The Mechanism

Page Views

        trackPageView(
          "/LearnChineseActivity");
          
        trackPageView(
          "/FlashCardActivity
            /Level/HSK1
              /Card/1");

Events

        trackEvent(
          "LearnChineseActivity",
            "Start",             
              "HSK1", 1);        
        
        trackEvent(
          "FlashCardActivity",
            "Swipe",
              "Next", 1);
        or
            "Click",
              "Random", 1);
